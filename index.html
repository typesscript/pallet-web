FAQs

Questions, meet answers.

What makes Pallet different from ServiceTitan?

Pallet is built for small to mid-size contractors who want automation without the complexity. Our AI agents handle calls, scheduling, dispatching, billing, and more — no extra hires, no steep learning curve.

Is Pallet a good fit for my business?

If you are under $2M/year in revenue, Pallet will you more value and faster adoption than enterprise platforms. When you hit $3M in revenue and feel you have outgrown Pallet, we will migrate you to ServiceTitan for free.

Do I need to change my business phone number?

No! Pallet provides a number you can forward calls to or integrate directly with your phone system. You can transfer all the incoming calls or only after-hour calls or calls to a particular location - your choice.

Why can't I build this service myself with AI coding assistants?

You can use something like ElevenLabs and build a voice AI agent in less than 30 minutes. And we encourage you to try it out if you are just starting with exploring AI agents for your services business. At some point, you will hit a limit and we are there for you to enhance the experience for your customers.

What if I'm locked-in with another vendor?

Lock-ins are bad. Period. That being said, we will buyout your contract. If you are 6 months into a 12-month contract, we will give you 18 months for the price of 12. If you just signed with a competitor, we will give you 24 for the price of 12.

Can I try Pallet before committing?

Yes — all plans come with a 14 days free trial so you can get a hang of how useful Pallet is.
